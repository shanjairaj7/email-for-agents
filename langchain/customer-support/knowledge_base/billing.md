# Billing & Subscription Policies

Everything you need to know about Beacon's pricing, payment, refunds, and subscription management.

---

## Subscription Tiers

### Starter — $29 / month

- Up to 10 team members
- 3 integrations
- 12 months of historical data
- Standard dashboards and reports
- Email support

### Growth — $99 / month

- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited integrations
- 12 months of historical data
- Custom dashboards, custom reports, REST API access
- CSV / PDF exports
- Email + live chat support

### Enterprise — custom pricing

- Everything in Growth
- 36 months of historical data
- Dedicated Slack channel + phone support
- SSO (SAML 2.0, Okta, Google Workspace)
- Custom SLA and uptime guarantee
- Audit logs
- Priority onboarding and quarterly business reviews

All prices are listed in USD. Annual plans are available at a **20% discount** (equivalent to ~2 months free). Contact sales@beacon.app for volume or multi-workspace pricing.

---

## Free Trial

All new workspaces begin with a **14-day free trial** of the Growth plan. No credit card is required to start. At the end of the trial, you'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you take no action, the workspace is downgraded to a read-only state for 7 days before data is removed.

---

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- ACH bank transfer (US accounts, annual plans only — contact billing@beacon.app)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans, invoice billing available)

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Beacon does not store card details directly. Your card is charged automatically at the start of each billing period.

---

## Billing Cycle

- **Monthly plans** — billed on the same calendar day each month (e.g. if you subscribed on the 15th, you're billed on the 15th of each month).
- **Annual plans** — billed once per year on your subscription anniversary date.

Invoices are emailed to the billing email on file and are also available in **Settings → Billing → Invoices**.

---

## Upgrades and Downgrades

### Upgrading

Upgrades take effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period, then the full new plan rate on the next renewal.

### Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period. You retain access to your current plan's features until then. After downgrading, features not included in the new plan (e.g. custom dashboards on Starter) are hidden but not deleted — they'll reappear if you upgrade again.

---

## Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time from **Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain full access until then.

**We do not offer partial-month refunds** for monthly plans. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access through the end of that billing cycle.

For **annual plans**, if you cancel within 30 days of your renewal date, you may be eligible for a prorated refund — see the Refund Policy below.

---

## Refund Policy

| Situation | Refund |
|---|---|
| Cancel within 14-day free trial | No charge, nothing to refund |
| Cancel a monthly plan | No refund — access continues until end of period |
| Cancel an annual plan within 30 days of purchase or renewal | Prorated refund for unused months |
| Cancel an annual plan after 30 days | No refund |
| Billing error (duplicate charge, incorrect amount) | Full refund, processed within 5–10 business days |
| Service outage exceeding SLA threshold (Enterprise) | Credit applied per SLA terms |

To request a refund, email billing@beacon.app with your workspace name and reason. We'll review and respond within 2 business days.

## Changing Your Billing Email or Card

- **Billing email**: Settings → Billing → Billing Email
- **Payment method**: Settings → Billing → Payment Method → Update Card

Only workspace Admins can access billing settings.

## Taxes

Prices shown are exclusive of applicable taxes. Depending on your location, VAT, GST, or sales tax may be added at checkout. US customers in states where software SaaS is taxable will see sales tax applied automatically.

## Frequently Asked Billing Questions

**Can I pause my subscription?**
We don't currently offer a pause feature. If you need to take a break, you can cancel and re-subscribe later — your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation.

**What happens to my data if I don't pay?**
After a failed payment we retry 3 times over 7 days. If payment still fails, the workspace is suspended (read-only). After 30 days of suspension without resolution, the workspace and its data are permanently deleted. We send email warnings at each stage.

**Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or startups?**
Yes — email billing@beacon.app with details. We offer 30–50% discounts for verified nonprofits and early-stage startups (under $1M ARR).

**Can I get a quote or purchase order for procurement?**
Yes. Contact sales@beacon.app for a formal quote, W-9 (US), or purchase order processing. Enterprise invoices can be net-30 or net-60 on request.
