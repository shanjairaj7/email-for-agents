## Billing Cycle

**When am I charged?**
Pro subscriptions are charged on the same day each month (or year) from the date you upgrade. For example, if you upgrade on the 14th, you are billed on the 14th of every subsequent month.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) processed via Stripe. ACH bank transfer is available for annual Enterprise contracts.

**Can I change my billing date?**
Billing dates are set at subscription start and cannot be changed. If you need a specific billing date, cancel your current subscription and resubscribe on the desired date.

**Where do I find my invoices?**
Go to **Billing → Invoices**. All invoices are listed with date, amount, and a PDF download link. Invoices are also emailed to the billing contact address on file.

**How do I update my payment method?**
Go to **Billing → Payment method** and click **Update card**. The new card takes effect immediately for future charges.

## Upgrades and Downgrades

**How do I upgrade to Pro?**
Go to **Billing → Plans** and click **Upgrade to Pro**. Enter your payment details. The Pro features activate immediately, and you are billed a prorated amount for the remaining days in the current month.

**Can I downgrade back to Free?**
Yes. Go to **Billing → Plans** and click **Downgrade to Free**. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period. At that point, your account is moved to Free. Projects exceeding the Free limits are locked (not deleted) until you reduce usage or re-upgrade.

**What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
Your data is preserved for 90 days after downgrading. After 90 days, locked projects and data exceeding Free limits are permanently deleted. You will receive email warnings at 30 days and 7 days before deletion.

## Refunds and Cancellations

**What is your refund policy?**
We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** for new Pro subscriptions. If you are not satisfied within the first 14 days, contact support@acme.io and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

After 14 days, monthly subscriptions are non-refundable. Annual subscriptions are eligible for a prorated refund (less any months already used) within the first 60 days. Contact support@acme.io to request one.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to **Billing → Plans → Cancel subscription**. You can also email support@acme.io and we will handle it for you. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period — you keep Pro access until then.

**Will I receive a confirmation when I cancel?**
Yes. You will receive a cancellation confirmation email immediately. If you do not receive it within 5 minutes, check spam and then contact support.

---

## Failed Payments

**What happens if my payment fails?**
We retry failed payments 3 times over 7 days (on days 1, 3, and 7 after the initial failure). You will receive an email notification after each failed attempt. If all retries fail, your account is downgraded to Free.

**How do I reactivate after a failed payment?**
Update your payment method under **Billing → Payment method**, then go to **Billing → Plans** and click **Reactivate Pro**. You will be charged immediately for the current billing period.

---

## Taxes

**Do you charge VAT / sales tax?**
For customers in the EU, we add VAT at the rate applicable to your country. For US customers, we collect sales tax in states where we have nexus. Your invoices show the tax amount separately.

**I have a VAT number — how do I add it?**
Go to **Billing → Tax information** and enter your VAT number. Once verified, future invoices will show it and VAT will be zero-rated for B2B transactions within the EU.

---

## Disputes and Chargebacks

If you have a concern about a charge, please contact support@acme.io before initiating a chargeback. We resolve billing disputes quickly and chargebacks create delays for both parties. Accounts with open chargebacks are suspended until the dispute is resolved.
